<p style="margin-top: -3px;margin-bottom: -3px;"><strong>ShinyEvents</strong> is a web-based analytical framework designed for the exploration and visualization of complex longitudinal clinical data with features such as:</p>
<ul style="margin-top: -3px;margin-bottom: -3px;">
  <li style="margin-top: -3px;">Interactive timelines that trace individual patient trajectories</li>
  <li>Treatment clustering, treatment duration, and endpoint annotation</li>
  <li>Intuitive visual summaries, including Sankey diagrams, Swimmer plots, and Kaplan-Meier plots</li>
  <li style="margin-bottom: -3px;">Explore real-world progression-free survival (rwPFS) based on user-defined clinical endpoints</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin-top: -3px;margin-bottom: -3px;">By linking a clinical journeys with patients' outcomes, ShinyEvents empowers real-time, multilayered longitudinal data analysis to support clinical research and decision-making.</p>
